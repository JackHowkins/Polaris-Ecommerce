Our Returns Policy


If you are not satisfied with all or certain of the products you have
ordered you may return them free of charge.

Simply utilise the pre-printed returns label inside your order and
drop off at your local Royal Mail drop-off location. Within the
package be sure to include the provided information form including
the reason for return and the products that you have chosen to return.

Once you have sent the return, it will arrive to us within the next
3 working days, we will let you know once we have recieved the parcel
to indicate that we are processing the return, once it is fully
processed we will once again contact you to let you know that the
refund has been sent. Depending on your bank provider, this then
should appear in your account within the next 7 working days.
