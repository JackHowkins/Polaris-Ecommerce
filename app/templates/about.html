About us


We are a streetwear apparel brand established in 2020 based out of
Leeds, United Kingdom. We offer t shirts, hats, hoodies and sweats
with our unique, bespoke designs. We are always designing and creating
new and exciting goods so keep an eye out!

Stay up to date on our social media channels such as Instagram, Facebook,
Twitter and more. See exclusive sneak peaks of new apparel and be in with
of a chance of winning FREE products every week!

Our Ethical Statement


We source all of materials from reliable and ethical suppliers.
We believe strongly about fairtrade and high quality working
conditions up and down our supply chain.

On top of this, we donate 5% of all of profits to wateraid and other
similar charities. As we believe, with our growth, we should help
contribute towards the world's wider
problems.
